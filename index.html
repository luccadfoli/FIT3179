Residential Usage of Solar Panels

Over the years, the use of solar panels (photovoltaic cells) on the top of roofs in residential areas has been increasing more and more. Roughly 38% of homes across Australia now generate their own solar power. South Australia and Queensland lead the transition with more than half of households equipped with rooftop solar systems. Western Australia follows closely at 46.98%.
